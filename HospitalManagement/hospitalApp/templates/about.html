About Us

Our disease prediction platform is a web-based tool that enables users to estimate their risk of breast cancer, diabetes, and heart disease by analyzing their symptoms, medical history, and other relevant details.

The system is built to offer accurate and dependable insights, empowering users to make well-informed health decisions. By leveraging advanced algorithms and machine learning, it processes large datasets to deliver personalized predictions tailored to each individual..

Diabetes

Diabetes is a condition in which the body is unable to regulate blood sugar levels. There are two main types of diabetes: type 1 diabetes and type 2 diabetes. Our system takes into account a user's age, weight, family history, and other factors to predict their risk of developing diabetes.
